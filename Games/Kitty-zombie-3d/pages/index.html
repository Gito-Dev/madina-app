HOW TO PLAY

Move Left stick / arrow keys — roam the map
Shoot Right stick — aim & auto-fire pink balls
Health — restores +50 HP
Speed Boot — run faster for 60s
Nuke — kills every zombie on screen
Armor — shield that soaks damage (chest only)
Gems — drop from zombies, kept in your inventory
Chest — open with 5 gold, 3 emerald or 1 ruby for buffs
Bag Tap an inventory slot, then USE to activate a buff
Portal Step on the green dungeon & press ENTER for the Biohazard zone
